MITIGATION

Mitigation is the action of reducing the severity, seriousness, or painfulness of something.

For Earthquakes, Brgy. Pembo, Makati City is in line with West Valley Faults The West Valley Fault is 100 kilometers in length and traverses the cities of Metro Manila namely Taguig, Muntinlupa, Paranaque, Quezon City, Pasig, Makati, and Marikina as well as nearby provinces of Rizal, Laguna, Cavite, and Bulacan. These places will suffer greatly when this fault moves. And when it did, it could generate a 7.2 magnitude earthquake causing great devastation of the most progressive portion of the Metro Manila.
